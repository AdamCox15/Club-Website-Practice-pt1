Information on Wasatch Hockey Club

Here at Wasatch Hockey Club we love everything that is hockey. We have different activies at our meetings. So wether you want to come work on your skating skills, puck control, shooting, or play a pick up game. We are the club for you!


Feel free to come in and check out our club. We meet at a few different ice rinks during the week. So if one rink is more convenient for you. There is still a chance to come play and practice the amazing sport of Hockey.


We have been around for 15 years and we are proud to be the premier hockey club on the Wasatch front. Some of our members are former NHL players, along with a variety of ECHL and AHL players. We also have entire families of members. So no matter what your age group, or skill level there are always people to play with and learn from.


* We offer ice time for individuals with special needs as well. Yes this includes games! *
